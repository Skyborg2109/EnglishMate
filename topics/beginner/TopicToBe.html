"To Be" adalah kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) yang sangat penting dalam bahasa Inggris. To Be memiliki tiga bentuk: am, is, are yang digunakan sesuai dengan subjek kalimat.

Subject To Be Contoh Kalimat
I am I am a student.
You are You are my friend.
He is He is a doctor.
She is She is beautiful.
It is It is a cat.
We are We are students.
They are They are happy.

ðŸ’¡ Penggunaan To Be

1. Positive Sentences (Kalimat Positif)

Formula: Subject + To Be + Complement

  • I am happy.
  • She is a teacher.
  • They are from Indonesia.
  • We are in the classroom.

2. Negative Sentences (Kalimat Negatif)

Formula: Subject + To Be + not + Complement

  • I am not (I'm not) tired.
  • He is not (isn't) a student.
  • They are not (aren't) at home.
  • It is not (isn't) expensive.

3. Question Sentences (Kalimat Tanya)

Formula: To Be + Subject + Complement?

  • Are you ready?
  • Is she your sister?
  • Am I late?
  • Are they students?
