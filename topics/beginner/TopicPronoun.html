Pronoun (kata ganti) adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menggantikan noun (kata benda) agar kalimat tidak berulang-ulang menyebutkan kata benda yang sama.

ðŸ‘¥ Jenis-Jenis Pronoun

1. Subject Pronouns (Kata Ganti Subjek)

Pronoun Arti Contoh Kalimat
I Saya I am a student.
You Kamu/Anda You are my friend.
He Dia (laki-laki) He is a teacher.
She Dia (perempuan) She is beautiful.
It Dia/Itu (benda/hewan) It is a cat.
We Kami/Kita We are students.
They Mereka They are happy.

2. Object Pronouns (Kata Ganti Objek)

Pronoun Arti Contoh Kalimat
me saya She loves me.
you kamu/anda I like you.
him dia (laki-laki) I know him.
her dia (perempuan) I help her.
it dia/itu (benda/hewan) I see it.
us kami/kita They visit us.
them mereka We meet them.

3. Possessive Pronouns (Kata Ganti Kepemilikan)

Pronoun Arti Contoh Kalimat
mine milik saya This book is mine.
yours milik kamu That pen is yours.
his milik dia (laki-laki) The car is his.
hers milik dia (perempuan) The bag is hers.
ours milik kami/kita The house is ours.
theirs milik mereka The toys are theirs.

  • I saw Mary yesterday. I gave her a gift. (Object Pronoun)
  • This is my book. That book is yours. (Possessive Pronoun)
  • We are going to the park. Do you want to join us?
