Modal Verbs adalah kata kerja bantu yang digunakan bersama kata kerja utama untuk menyatakan kemampuan, kemungkinan, izin, saran, kewajiban, atau kepastian.

ðŸ’¡ Penggunaan Modal Verbs

1. CAN / COULD - Kemampuan & Izin

Kemampuan (Ability):

  • I can speak English. (Saya bisa berbahasa Inggris)
  • She could swim when she was 5. (Dia bisa berenang saat umur 5)

Izin (Permission):

  • Can I use your phone? (Bolehkah saya pakai HP-mu?)
  • Could you help me? (Bisakah kamu membantu saya?)

2. MAY / MIGHT - Kemungkinan & Izin

Kemungkinan (Possibility):

  • It may rain tomorrow. (Mungkin akan hujan besok)
  • She might be late. (Dia mungkin terlambat)

Izin Formal (Permission):

  • May I come in? (Bolehkah saya masuk?)

3. WILL / WOULD - Masa Depan & Kesediaan

  • I will help you. (Saya akan membantu kamu)
  • Would you like some coffee? (Apakah kamu mau kopi?)
  • I would go if I had time. (Saya akan pergi jika punya waktu)

4. SHOULD / OUGHT TO - Saran & Kewajiban

  • You should study harder. (Kamu sebaiknya belajar lebih giat)
  • We ought to help them. (Kita seharusnya membantu mereka)
  • You should not (shouldn't) smoke. (Kamu sebaiknya tidak merokok)

5. MUST - Keharusan & Kepastian

Keharusan (Obligation):

  • You must wear a seatbelt. (Kamu harus pakai sabuk pengaman)
  • Students must do their homework. (Siswa harus mengerjakan PR)

Kepastian (Certainty):

  • She must be tired. (Dia pasti lelah)
  • It must be expensive. (Itu pasti mahal)

  • cannot (can't) - tidak bisa
  • could not (couldn't) - tidak bisa (masa lalu)
  • may not - mungkin tidak / tidak boleh
  • must not (mustn't) - tidak boleh (larangan)
  • should not (shouldn't) - sebaiknya tidak

ðŸ’¡ Perbedaan Penting:

must not = larangan (tidak boleh)

don't have to = tidak perlu (tidak wajib)

Contoh: You must not smoke here. (Kamu tidak boleh merokok di sini)

Contoh: You don't have to come. (Kamu tidak perlu datang)
