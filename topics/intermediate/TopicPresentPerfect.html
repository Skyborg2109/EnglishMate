Present Perfect Tense

INTERMEDIATE LEVEL

Pengertian

Present Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang sudah selesai di masa lampau tetapi masih ada hubungannya dengan masa sekarang, atau kejadian yang dimulai di masa lampau dan masih berlanjut sampai sekarang.

Formula

Positive: Subject + have/has + Verb 3 + Object

Negative: Subject + have/has + not + Verb 3 + Object

Question: Have/Has + Subject + Verb 3 + Object?

Gunakan has untuk: He, She, It

Gunakan have untuk: I, You, We, They

Contoh Kalimat

Positive Sentences:

  • I have finished my homework.
  • She has lived in Jakarta for 5 years.
  • They have visited Bali three times.
  • We have known each other since 2010.

Negative Sentences:

  • I have not (haven't) seen that movie.
  • He has not (hasn't) eaten breakfast yet.
  • They have not (haven't) arrived yet.

Question Sentences:

  • Have you been to Paris?
  • Has she finished her work?
  • How long have you studied English?

° Kapan Digunakan?

  • Pengalaman hidup (tanpa waktu spesifik)
  • Kejadian yang baru saja terjadi
  • Kejadian yang dimulai di masa lalu dan masih berlanjut
  • Kejadian yang sudah selesai tapi hasilnya masih terlihat sekarang

Time Signals

Kata keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan:

  • just (baru saja), already (sudah), yet (belum/sudah)
  • ever (pernah), never (tidak pernah)
  • for (selama - durasi), since (sejak - titik waktu)
  • recently, lately (akhir-akhir ini)
  • so far, up to now (sampai sekarang)

For vs Since

FOR - Digunakan untuk durasi waktu:

  • I have lived here for 3 years.
  • She has worked there for 6 months.

SINCE - Digunakan untuk titik waktu mulai:

  • I have lived here since 2020.
  • She has worked there since January.

Tips:

Jangan gunakan waktu spesifik (yesterday, last week, in 2010) dengan Present Perfect!
