Past Continuous Tense

INTERMEDIATE LEVEL

Pengertian

Past Continuous Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang sedang berlangsung di waktu tertentu di masa lampau atau kejadian yang sedang berlangsung ketika kejadian lain terjadi.

Formula

Positive: Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + Object

Negative: Subject + was/were + not + Verb-ing + Object

Question: Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing + Object?

Gunakan was untuk: I, He, She, It

Contoh Kalimat

Positive Sentences:

  • I was studying English at 8 PM yesterday.
  • She was cooking dinner when I arrived.
  • They were playing football in the park.
  • We were watching TV all evening.

Negative Sentences:

  • I was not (wasn't) sleeping at that time.
  • He was not (wasn't) working yesterday.
  • They were not (weren't) listening to the teacher.

Question Sentences:

  • Was she reading a book?
  • Were you studying last night?
  • What were they doing at 9 PM?

  • Dua kejadian yang terjadi bersamaan di masa lalu
  • Kejadian yang sedang berlangsung ketika kejadian lain terjadi (dengan Simple Past)
  • Untuk memberikan latar belakang cerita

Time Signals

Kata keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan:

  • at 8 o'clock yesterday, at that time, at that moment
  • when, while, as
  • all day yesterday, all morning, all evening
  • this time last week/month/year

Past Continuous vs Simple Past

Perhatikan perbedaannya:

  • I was reading when he called. (sedang membaca ketika dia menelepon)
  • While I was cooking, she was studying. (dua kejadian bersamaan)
  • They were playing football when it started to rain.
