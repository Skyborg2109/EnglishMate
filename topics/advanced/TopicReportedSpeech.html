Reported Speech

ADVANCED LEVEL

Pengertian

Reported Speech (Indirect Speech) adalah cara melaporkan atau menceritakan kembali apa yang dikatakan orang lain tanpa menggunakan kutipan langsung. Berbeda dengan Direct Speech yang menggunakan tanda kutip.

  • Direct Speech: She said, "I am happy."
  • Reported Speech: She said (that) she was happy.

Perubahan Tense (Backshift)

Ketika reporting verb (said, told) dalam bentuk past, tense dalam kalimat berubah mundur satu tingkat.

Reporting Statements (Pernyataan)

Formula: Subject + said/told + (that) + clause

  • Direct: "I love English," she said.
  • Reported: She said (that) she loved English.
  • Direct: "I am studying," he said.
  • Reported: He said (that) he was studying.
  • Direct: "I will come tomorrow," Tom said.
  • Reported: Tom said (that) he would come the next day.

Yes/No Questions

Formula: Subject + asked + if/whether + clause

  • Direct: "Are you happy?" she asked.
  • Reported: She asked if/whether I was happy.
  • Direct: "Do you like coffee?" he asked.
  • Reported: He asked if/whether I liked coffee.

Wh- Questions

Formula: Subject + asked + wh-word + clause

  • Direct: "Where do you live?" she asked.
  • Reported: She asked where I lived.
  • Direct: "What are you doing?" he asked.
  • Reported: He asked what I was doing.

Reporting Commands (Perintah)

Formula: Subject + told/asked + object + (not) to + verb

  • Direct: "Close the door," she said.
  • Reported: She told me to close the door.
  • Direct: "Don't be late," he said.
  • Reported: He told me not to be late.
  • Direct: "Please help me," she said.
  • Reported: She asked me to help her.
