Relative Clauses (Adjective Clauses) adalah klausa yang memberikan informasi tambahan tentang noun (kata benda). Dimulai dengan relative pronoun: who, whom, whose, which, that.

that Orang/Benda/Hewan Subjek/Objek

ðŸ’¡ Jenis Relative Clauses

1. Defining Relative Clauses

Memberikan informasi penting/esensial untuk mengidentifikasi noun. Tidak menggunakan koma.

  • The man who lives next door is a doctor.
  • The book that I bought yesterday is interesting.
  • The car which is parked outside is mine.
  • Students who study hard will succeed.

2. Non-Defining Relative Clauses

Memberikan informasi tambahan/tidak esensial. Menggunakan koma dan tidak bisa menggunakan "that".

  • My brother, who lives in London, is a teacher.
  • The Eiffel Tower, which is in Paris, is very famous.
  • John, whose sister I know, is coming to the party.

ðŸŽ¯ Penggunaan Relative Pronouns

WHO - Untuk Orang (Subjek)

  • The woman who called you is my sister.
  • I know a man who speaks five languages.

WHOM - Untuk Orang (Objek)

  • The man whom I met yesterday is a doctor.
  • The girl whom you saw is my friend.

ðŸ’¡ Tips: Dalam bahasa informal, "whom" sering diganti dengan "who" atau dihilangkan:

The man I met yesterday is a doctor. (whom dihilangkan)

WHOSE - Untuk Kepemilikan

  • The girl whose bag was stolen is crying.
  • I know a man whose son is a famous actor.
  • The house whose roof is red belongs to me.

WHICH - Untuk Benda/Hewan

  • The book which is on the table is mine.
  • I like the dress which you are wearing.
  • The dog which barked all night is gone.

THAT - Universal (Orang/Benda)

  • The man that lives next door is friendly.
  • The car that I bought is fast.
  • This is the best movie that I have ever seen.

Relative pronoun bisa dihilangkan jika berfungsi sebagai objek dalam defining relative clause:

  • The book (which/that) I read was interesting.
  • The man (whom/that) I met was kind.
  • The movie (which/that) we watched was boring.
