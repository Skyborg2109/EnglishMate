Passive Voice

ADVANCED LEVEL

Pengertian

Passive Voice adalah bentuk kalimat di mana subjek menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Fokus kalimat adalah pada objek yang dikenai aksi, bukan pelaku aksi.

Formula Umum

Active: Subject + Verb + Object

Passive: Object + be + Verb 3 + (by Subject)

Contoh Perubahan:

  • Active: She writes a letter.
  • Passive: A letter is written by her.

Future Perfect She will have written a letter. A letter will have been written by her.

Passive Voice dengan Modal Verbs

Formula: Modal + be + Verb 3

  • Active: They can solve the problem.
  • Passive: The problem can be solved by them.
  • Active: You must finish the work.
  • Passive: The work must be finished by you.
  • Active: She should clean the room.
  • Passive: The room should be cleaned by her.

Kapan Menggunakan Passive Voice?

  • Ketika pelaku aksi tidak diketahui atau tidak penting
  • Untuk menekankan objek yang dikenai aksi
  • Dalam penulisan formal atau ilmiah
  • Ketika pelaku aksi sudah jelas dari konteks

Contoh Penggunaan

Pelaku tidak diketahui:

  • My car was stolen last night. (Tidak tahu siapa yang mencuri)
  • The window has been broken. (Tidak tahu siapa yang memecahkan)

Menekankan objek:

  • The Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo da Vinci.
  • English is spoken in many countries.

Penulisan formal:

  • The experiment was conducted in 2020.
  • The results will be published next month.

Active vs Passive

  • Active: The teacher explains the lesson. (Fokus: guru)
  • Passive: The lesson is explained by the teacher. (Fokus: pelajaran)
