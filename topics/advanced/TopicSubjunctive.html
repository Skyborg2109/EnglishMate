Subjunctive Mood adalah bentuk kata kerja khusus yang digunakan untuk menyatakan keinginan, saran, permintaan, atau situasi yang tidak nyata. Berbeda dengan indicative mood (pernyataan fakta) dan imperative mood (perintah).

Dalam subjunctive, verb selalu dalam bentuk base form (infinitive tanpa to) untuk semua subject, termasuk third person singular (he, she, it).

ðŸ’¡ Penggunaan Subjunctive

1. Setelah Verbs of Demand/Request/Suggestion

Kata kerja: suggest, recommend, propose, insist, demand, request, ask, require, order

Formula: Subject + verb + that + subject + base form

  • I suggest that he study harder.
  • The doctor recommends that she take this medicine.
  • They insist that we be on time.
  • She demands that he leave immediately.
  • The teacher requires that all students attend the class.

2. Setelah Adjectives of Importance/Necessity

Kata sifat: important, essential, necessary, vital, crucial, imperative

Formula: It is + adjective + that + subject + base form

  • It is important that he be here on time.
  • It is essential that she finish the project.
  • It is necessary that we arrive early.
  • It is vital that they understand the rules.
  • It is crucial that everyone participate.

3. Dalam Ekspresi Formal/Wishes

  • God save the Queen!
  • Long live the King!
  • Heaven forbid!
  • God bless you!
  • So be it.

4. Dengan "If I were" (Unreal Conditional)

Untuk semua subject, gunakan "were" (bukan "was") dalam conditional type 2.

  • If I were you, I would accept the offer.
  • If he were here, he would help us.
  • If she were rich, she would travel the world.
  • I wish I were taller.
  • He acts as if he were the boss.
